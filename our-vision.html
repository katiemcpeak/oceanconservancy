Our Visions

What we want to see when our hard work is complete

Clean Beaches, Clean Water

We’re organizing beach cleanups around the globe, providing the science behind ocean debris and collaborating with everyone from citizens like you to businesses to stop ocean trash at the source.

A Thriving Ocean Economy

Ocean Conservancy works to protect the ocean for those who depend on it to make their living. We’re working to secure sustainable fishing and a robust tourist industry.

Healthy and Sustainable Food

A healthy ocean provides us with the food we eat, the water we drink and the air we breathe. Ocean Conservancy works to protect the ocean so it can sustain communities around the world.

A Balanced, Working Ocean

Ocean Conservancy uses science to create policies that protect the ocean's habitats and wildlife, while providing space for communities and economies to prosper.

Vibrant Marine Life

Life on Earth began in the sea, and today ocean life continues to nurture and sustain all of us, regardless of where we live.

Why The Ocean Matters

Every second breath you take comes from the ocean. No matter where you live, your life depends on the ocean. Beyond the breaths you take, the ocean plays a critical role in regulating our weather and climate.
